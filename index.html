FAQs

Frequently asked questions

How do you verify advocates?
We verify each advocate's Bar Council enrolment number and confirm active standing before their profile goes live. Profiles display the enrolment state and year so you can cross-check independently.
How much does a consultation cost?
Each advocate sets a fixed consultation fee, shown on their profile before you book. Typical range is ₹500–₹3,000. Asking a question on the Q&A forum is always free.
Is my information confidential?
Yes. Case details are shared only with the advocate you book. Q&A questions are posted anonymously by default.
What if I'm not satisfied with the consultation?
Your first consultation is covered by our refund guarantee — if it doesn't meet expectations, contact us within 24 hours for a full refund.
Can I reschedule or cancel?
You can reschedule or cancel free of charge up to 4 hours before the scheduled slot, directly from your booking confirmation.

Company & policies

About LegalConnects

LegalConnects is a legal information and technology platform that helps people across India find Bar Council–verified advocates, ask legal questions, track court cases, and book consultations with transparent, fixed fees. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice; every consultation is between you and an independent advocate. Every advocate on the platform is verified against Bar Council records before their profile goes live, visibility is never sold, and client reviews cannot be bought or edited. LegalConnects is operated from Delhi, India. Reach us at info@legalconnects.in.

Privacy Policy

We collect only the information needed to provide the service: your account details, and — when you book a consultation or post a case — your contact details and case description. Case details are shared only with the advocate(s) you engage, never published or sold. Q&A questions are posted anonymously by default. You may request access to or deletion of your personal data at any time by writing to info@legalconnects.in, and we will act on such requests in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023. Payment information is processed by Razorpay and never stored on our servers.

Terms & Conditions

LegalConnects is a technology platform connecting users with independent advocates. We are not a party to any advocate-client engagement and do not guarantee outcomes. Content on the Q&A forum is general legal information, not legal advice. Advocates are solely responsible for their profiles and answers and must comply with the Bar Council of India Rules, including Rule 36; the platform does not offer advertising, rankings, or paid placement. Misuse — including fake profiles, solicitation, or posting others' personal information — leads to suspension.

Refund Policy

Your first consultation is covered by our satisfaction guarantee: if it does not take place, or does not meet reasonable expectations, contact us within 24 hours at info@legalconnects.in for a full refund of the consultation fee. Consultations cancelled or rescheduled more than 4 hours before the slot are free of charge; refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 working days via Razorpay.
